4corners

Political Compass

What is 4corners?

The 4corners political compass test, similar to the other political compass tests, is a series of 50 to 100 (to the best of our ability) unbiased questions, that can be used to place your political views onto a visual chart.

What makes it different?

While the presentation of the results may seem similar to the commonly found political compass tests, 4corners uses a completely new algorithm to create those results, using data such as the current Overton Window to hopefully provide a largely more accurate final plot.

Anything else I need to know?
